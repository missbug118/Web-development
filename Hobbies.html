cricket

Cricket involves two teams competing to score runs by hitting the ball and defending wickets. Matches vary from short Twenty20s to long Test games lasting five days. Players specialize as batsmen, bowlers, or fielders, making teamwork and strategy essential for victory, while fans passionately follow their favorite teams.

football

Football, played globally, requires agility, coordination, and communication. Each team strives to score goals while defending their own. The game fosters discipline, sportsmanship, and passion, bringing communities together. From local fields to international stadiums, football inspires players of all ages, uniting people through excitement and competition.

badminton

Badminton requires players to hit the shuttlecock over a net, aiming to land it in the opponent’s court. It can be played in singles or doubles, enhancing fitness, coordination, and teamwork while providing fun and competitive excitement.

basket ball

Basketball is a fast-paced team sport where players aim to score by shooting a ball through a hoop. It requires skill, teamwork, and strategy. Played worldwide, it develops physical fitness and coordination. Dribbling, passing, and shooting are essential, while teamwork and communication drive success on the court.
