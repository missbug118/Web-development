facts about space

Space is a vast, mysterious expanse beyond Earth’s atmosphere, filled with stars, planets, galaxies, and cosmic wonders. It inspires exploration, fuels scientific discovery, and challenges human imagination. From black holes to supernovas, space reveals the universe’s grandeur and reminds us how small yet curious we truly are.

Space is ancient and immense, holding countless galaxies, stars, and mysteries. The universe is 13.8 billion years old, still expanding, and mostly made of dark matter and dark energy. Humans have only touched the Moon, yet cosmic wonders like black holes remind us how much remains unknown.
